Economic Stability Reclass

The 30 economic stability categories used by Mass Mutual were reclassed into three categories: "High" (1-10), "Medium" (11-20) and "Low" (21-30). The fidelity was reduced to generate clear economic stability signatures below.

    Building Economic Stability Signatures

    Broadly speaking, MassMutual's customer base is mostly made up of those of Medium economic stability, followed by those of High and Low economic stability respectively.

    Since economic stability is the second strongest determinant of customer segmentation, building clear signatures on economic stability for each segment can help identify areas of differentiation needed to address contrasting needs and wants.
